# Summary

I've tried to make transmission filter by thin printing, t0.6mm and t1.2mm.
The picture with a GAME&WATCH and a keyboard is Face_t0.6. I feel its transparency is a bit too high.
Another is Face_t1.2 and the actual is better than the picture.

Printer: R1 ABS + PLA Model
Rafts: No
Supports: Doesn't Matter
Resolution: 0.1mm for Face
Infill: as you like

In the sample image, I used PETG for all, clear for face, red for two button, black for others.
